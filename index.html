🌱 The Problem

Manual mango sorting is time-consuming, inconsistent, and prone to human error. Farmers and distributors lose revenue due to inaccurate ripeness detection and wastage.

🤖 Our Solution

Our AI-powered detector uses sensors and image recognition to determine the exact ripeness stage of each mango, ensuring better quality control and higher market value.

🔍 How It Works

1. Image Capture

Each mango is photographed or scanned under controlled lighting.

2. AI Analysis

Machine learning model analyzes color, texture & pattern to assess ripeness.

3. Classification

The mango is tagged as raw, semi-ripe, or ripe with high accuracy.
